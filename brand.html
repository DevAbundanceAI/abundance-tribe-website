The Gradient

Blue gradient · 45° · dark to light

Every gradient across the site follows the same rules: 45° angle, dark blue on the bottom-left to light blue on the top-right. Three blue stops — no washed-out blue-400.

Typography

Two fonts. One system.

Greycliff CF handles everything — body, headlines, labels, stats, UI. Neo Contact is used rarely, only for accent emphasis words inside headlines. Never both in the same moment.

Headlines · Usage Rules

How headlines are structured

The default headline is solid matte black Greycliff. Gradient appears only on specific elements: inline accent words via .gradient, or Neo Contact ems on their own line.

Default · Solid matte black

Scale your business with consistent new clients every month.

Standard H1/H2/H3. Greycliff Bold 700, solid matte black (#141414). Most headlines on the site use this style.

With inline gradient accent

Scale your business with Meta Ads — done for you.

Use <span class="gradient"> to color specific words inline. MultiplyClients-style accent.

With Neo Contact emphasis · on its own line

Install an AI-Powered Selling System that fills your calendar.

Use <em> for the BIG accent moment. Auto-renders Neo Contact, gradient color, 1.2× size, on its own line. Use rarely — once per page max.

Corner radius · 20px standard

All cards, containers, and highlighted text use 20px radius. Exceptions: buttons (10px, distinct element), pill labels (100px, intentional pill shape).
