The Gradient

Blue gradient · 45° · dark to light

Every gradient follows the same rules: 45° angle, deep navy-blue on the bottom-left to saturated bright blue on the top-right. Three stops.

Typography

Two fonts. One system.

Greycliff CF handles everything — body, headlines, labels, stats, UI. Neo Contact is used rarely, only for accent emphasis words inside headlines.

Headlines · Usage Rules

How headlines are structured

Default headline is solid matte black Greycliff. Gradient appears only on specific elements: inline accent via .gradient, or Neo Contact ems on their own line.

Default · Solid matte black

Scale your business with consistent new clients every month.

Standard H1/H2/H3. Greycliff Bold 700, solid matte black (#141414).

With inline gradient accent

Scale your business with Meta Ads — done for you.

Use <span class="gradient"> to color specific words inline.

With Neo Contact emphasis · on its own line

Install an AI-Powered Selling System that fills your calendar.

Use <em> for the BIG accent moment. Use rarely — once per page max.
