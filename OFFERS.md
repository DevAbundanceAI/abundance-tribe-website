## The Transformation Arc

The naming system tells a complete emotional journey:

**Tribe → Shift → Unlocked → Freedom → Partner**

- **Tribe** — You find clarity and community. You realize what's missing.
- **Shift** — Everything changes. How you think, how you operate, how you see your business. You build the foundation. You stop being the bottleneck.
- **Unlocked** — The system is running. The ceiling comes off. You're scaling.
- **Freedom** — You're there. Business runs. Life is yours. Peer-level room.
- **Partner** — Embedded. Done-with-you. Exclusive relationship.

## Naming History / Decisions

Names confirmed and why:
- **Shift** — chosen because the $6k transformation is fundamentally a mindset and operational shift before anything else. Not yet scaled, just changed.
- **Unlocked** — the system is built from Shift, now the ceiling comes off. Earned by the journey.
- **Freedom** — the destination. At $60k people aren't buying a peer group, they're buying what it represents. Freedom at the top is earned not promised.
- **Partner** — standalone, not part of the ladder. Signals a different relationship category.

Names considered and rejected:
- Accelerator — too overused in coaching space
- Pro — feels like a software tier
- Operator — strong but didn't fully land
- Forge, Edge, Capital, Apex — considered, didn't fit the arc
- Mastermind — conventional, Freedom felt more earned and specific
